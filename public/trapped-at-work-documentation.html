📋 What Is "Trapped at Work"?

NYC law requires employers to provide meals to employees who work overnight shifts (generally 11pm-7am) and cannot leave the workplace. Requirements include: Meal must be provided at no cost to employee, documentation of meal provision, alternative arrangements if employee declines.

Penalties: Fines per violation, DCWP enforcement actions.
