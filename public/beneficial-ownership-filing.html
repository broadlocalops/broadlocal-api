📋 NY LLC Transparency Act — Who Must File

Domestic NY LLCs (formed in NY or any US state): EXEMPT. No filing required.

Foreign LLCs (non-US entities doing business in NY): MUST FILE. Deadline Dec 30, 2026 for existing entities.

Penalties for non-compliance: $500 per day fines, loss of authority to do business in NY, delinquent status on public record.
